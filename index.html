Charity

Modern solution

An important cornerstone of the Ribbit.me mission is the empowerment of individuals to enable positive social change in the world, especially to unbanked and under-banked markets.

Giving back to the community is very important to us, said CEO Sean Dennis. "That is why a percentage of all RibbitRewards created will go to charity. We also have partnered with Melotic and Global Impact to allow charities around the world to accept donations in RibbitRewards."
